I encourage, support, and coach designers.

At 🤖 thoughtbot, I'm working to create an environment for designers to be fulfilled in their work. I iteratively plan, design, test, and build digital products. I rely on tools like Jobs-to-be-Done to work through complex problems and come up with appropriate solutions.

Personal Values

These values define who I would like to be 😀 and what is personally important. I revisit these values at the end of every year to recommit to myself who I am.

  • Be sustainable

    Maintain energy and patience to build and strengthen relationships. Life is a marathon not a sprint, but make the best use of time that you’ve been gifted.

  • Teach, mentor & promote

    Give opportunities to those that might not have had them. 📢 Amplify voices that might have otherwise gone unheard.

  • Always iterate

    Nothing is ever flawless, and nothing is ever done. There is always room to grow and improve in every aspect of life. Do not rely on anyone else to set your direction or hold you accountable.

  • Contribute to something larger

    What you decide to work on and who you work on it will define who you are and will define the community around you. 🏕 Leave everything better than you found it.

  • Simple is imperfect

    Add purposefully and remove when possible. Avoid justification of the new and see the value in what you already have. Embrace inconsitancies, mistakes, and flaws.
