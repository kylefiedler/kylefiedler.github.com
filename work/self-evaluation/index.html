Supporting Designers leveling up with the Skill Self-Evaluation

03/2019

  • Design Leadership
  • Management
  • Skill Development

thoughtbot Designers are generalists. They can work directly with stakeholders on design thinking workshops and turn around and work in code. Because the role is broad, Designers struggled with identifying areas of growth and identifying weaknesses.

I wanted to have a tool that Designers could see the progress that they were making in their skill growth and give them more direction and focus on skill development.
