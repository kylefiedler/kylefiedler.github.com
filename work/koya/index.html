Spreading kindness with friends and family through KOYA.

10–12/2018

  • Design Sprint
  • Visual Design

KOYA was created to bring people together through kindness that couldn't be in person. With a twist of surprise and delight with everyday activities. Buy your mom, in New York, a coffee when you're in Austin. Buy your best friend a drink in Philly when you live in Boston.

The founders of KOYA approached us first with a Design Sprint to help them validated their ideas and design a prototype. We then re-engaged for short bursts of design work as they built out their MVP.
