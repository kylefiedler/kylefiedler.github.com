Un-alive plants

It’s not all sunshine and rainbows here. I’ve killed my fair share of plants too. I’d like to think that each one has tought me a lesson so that others may survive.

  • ZZ Plant

    Turns out that watering consistently on the same day every week isn’t great.

  • Peace Lilly

    I wanted you to grow and put you in too big of a pot. Too big of a pot meant that you were sitting in pockets of wet soil too often.

  • Hybiscus

    In Texas we have a strong sun; you fell victim to that sun.

  • Majesty Palm

    Let’s be real, I was never going to be able to give you the right conditions for you to live.

  • Moon Cactus

    I learned that you were designed to not live long. That you’re really two plants, one sucking the life slowly out of the other.

  • Crocodile Fern

    So many pots don’t have drainage but they look so cool, right?

  • Alocasia Black Velvet

    All that peat moss that you were in coming from the store does a great job when you’re in that hot and humid greenhouse but not really so great in my dry home.
