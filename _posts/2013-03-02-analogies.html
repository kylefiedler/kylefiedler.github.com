I&rswuo;m getting quite tired of analogies for how we as web designers work. Maybe its just the context that I always hear them in but they always seem whiney. Designing for a screen is different. It’s not print design, it’s not product design, it’s not architecture. We can and should learn a lot from these industries but we shouldn’t rely on them to dictate our profession.

Designing for a screen is different. The sooner that you accept that the sooner we can move on as an industry. Stop complaining. Start doing.
