It’s scary being told how the odds are stocked up against you, that you probably can’t follow through or a project is simply impossible. It can either be a powerful motivator or intimidate the hell out of me to the point that I don’t want to do it. In both cases its a good thing. I either pour myself into the project or realize that its never going to see itself to completion and stop. It’s usually those projects that are the hardest complete that are the most rewarding and if I stop it allows me to put time towards another project.

When I told my wife what my goals for the frequency of posts were for this new blog, she told me I was better off trying to do much less. Basically dialing down the effort needed to maintain the . I’ll admit that this isn’t going to be easy for me. That at times I’m going to have to force myself to sit down and write for 15 minutes everyday. I know I’m going to hit a point where I don’t want to do it but I’m hoping that I will trudge along. After all writing a couple paragraphs every day isn’t that big of a challenge.
