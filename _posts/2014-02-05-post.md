We’ve lost power this morning and it made me instantly aware how dependent we are on it. No wifi, no cable, no coffee or tea, no heat. That last one is a kicker. I wonder if there is anything that we rely on more than electricity. I wonder how often we take advantage of this fact. One thing is for sure it will make us look at the world a little bit differently. At least till it comes back on.
