Focus is something that is becoming much harder these days. I feel like I have so many things going on with my life that finding focus is a struggle. Maybe part of it is bacause I work at home but I don't thing so. I think this abs always been a problem. It's an affect of carrying around an iPhone, being on the Internet all the time, having a full time job and a family life that includes a baby and puppy (god what were we thinking). It's that plus everything else that comes with daily life. I feel like my ancestors had so much less to worry about and do.

I know that this has got to be true because I see it in my wife. She's notoriously focused individual and lately I feel like she's lost her edge. She jumps from one topic to the next quickly and isn't balancing everything as well as she used to.

I think the easy way out is to blame the baby. He's caused quite the chaos in our lives and he certainly provides new needs that 4 months in still feel new. I'm still waiting for us to fall into a rhythm with him but it doesn't seem to be in the cards. Babies aren't meant to be on a schedule. For that matter I don't think anyone is meant to be on the schedules that we put ourselves on. So why exactly do we do it?
