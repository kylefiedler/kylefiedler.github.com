09/01/2011

It's been almost two years since my last post on this site. Over that time I've been engaged and gotten married, started a new job (it's not new anymore), moved twice, started a business with a friend and done a ton of design. That’s a lot. It explains to me why something had to go. Unfortunately, it was this site. It's not that during that time I didn't want to write for this site it's just that I didn't make time for it.

Insanity is doing the same thing over and over again and expecting different results.

I love that quote from Albert Einstein and it pretty much sums up what I am doing here in one word. For the third time I am restarting a blog expecting to actually stay with it this time. Crazy. Probably. But its something that I can just not let die, at least not yet.

Last time, I set out with expectations for both myself and the site. I set goals for myself to post on a regular basis to continue to update it and to never let it get stale. Of course I missed one monthly post and it was all down hill from there. This time I don’t want to set goals except to have some fun and maybe learn a little.

Oh and enjoy some vintage AeroSmith…
