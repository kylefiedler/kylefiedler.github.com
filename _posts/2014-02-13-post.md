On the train ride home today I eaves dropped on the persons phone call that was sitting next to me. He was explaining to someone how to make a really good fire. Since I’ve grown up with a fireplace and was a boy scout most of what he said wasn’t new for me. For the person on the other end of the phone thought they were clearly all new and very scary things to do. Hey fire can be quite dangerous.

It got me thinking that previous experiences dictate so much of how future experiences will happen. I’m much more likely to enjoy a fire in my fireplace than that person just because I’ve have previous experience doing it. More than that though it made me things about what experiences I am missing out on because I am afraid to start or didn’t have that experience before.
