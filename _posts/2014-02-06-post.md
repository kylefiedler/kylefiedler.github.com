We've been without power for more than 36 hours. It has been hell. We're now at our second home so that we can sleep in heat. We've had to travel 3 hours north. I've missed out on two full days of work. It's aggravating and frustrating but we've managed to do it and maintain a slight level of sanity throughout.

My dad watches the news every night. Something that I rarely do. Tonight there was someone from Syria who had just barely survived being gassed and having an extremely traumatic experience seeing other around her have he same experience. On top of that she had already lost her brother and her father from gunfire from the civil war.

Sometimes it's nice to have a little perspective.
