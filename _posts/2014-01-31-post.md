My biggest fear with starting this again is being vulnerable. I fear being taken as. Its one of the hardest things when starting something good or starting something that you think that you aren’t good at. I very well could be painting a poor portrait of who I am with all of these posts. I’ve been told by many people that their reaction to my writing is usually along the lines of “how can someone as smart as you write so bad?”.

I’ve tried to instill the opposite feeling within my daughter. That it’s ok to be bad at something and everyone needs to start from somewhere. Failure is a part of life and it’s the best way to learn. What I haven’t learned is that it’s easier to give that advice that actually follow through with it again and again.
