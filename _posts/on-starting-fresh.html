On starting fresh

It's been almost two years since I last posted on this site. Two fucking years. I've got tons of excuses but none of them really matter. All that matters is that I am finally back trying my best to write again.

The best part of starting fresh is that you get to rethink every little detail. You get to look back and really critisise what you did and how you can make it better. Then make it better.

Heres to fresh starts.
