The super bowl is an amazing phenomenon. You have a majority of americans watching the same show on TV. This ends up being a marketers dream. They get to show their very best ideas to a huge possible client base. Influence their “brand” over them. What I don’t get is that all of these people are so different that you are wasting lots of marketing money talking to the wrong people.

Then you have a select few that enjoy not watching it and not participating in the event. I’d figure that the best marketing could take place to these people since they would all be much more similar than those that are watching.

But what do I know, I certainly will never work at a company that will have the budget to advertise during the super bowl.
