I’ve had this stigma that in order to write I need to have a clear thought to sit down and write about. That I needed have everything worked out in my head before I put it down on paper or screen. Like somehow I couldn’t delete the words or edit them or change them. They were permanent.

Since I never really had anything that I felt I needed to say I just avoided writing all together. Why write something that didn’t mean anything, that had no concept behind it, no thought process. I’ve started to figure out that writing is part of the process. I need to sit down and write because it will help lead me to something not just help me communicate once I have something to say.
