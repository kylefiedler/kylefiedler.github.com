Spending a night away from my family feels wired. I love them all so much and spend so much time with them that when I'm not with them I mentally think something is wrong. I feel so blessed that I get to think this way. That my job and position allows me to spend so much time with them.

The best part about spending time away from them is realizing home much time I get to spend with them. Being able to cherish that time and spend that time is worth so much more than I am willing to give. It makes the time I spend awesome too because it makes me make sure that I am having fun when I'm not with them. It makes me value my time so much more.
