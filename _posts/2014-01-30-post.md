The thing that I have learned the most since moving from Boston is: just ask. I've had lots of lunches and coffees with really great people in Philly. All of whom shouldn't have had the time or care to meet up with me. I certainly haven't done anything amazing to deserve their time and attention. Yet almost every one has said yes. This has come as quite the surprise to me.

I would think that busy people wouldn't want to talk with me. What could they hope to benefit from this? I can tell you that it's not my stunning good looks or amazingly charming personality. I think part of it is my perception is different than who these people really are. The biggest reason has just got to be the fact that they are genuinely nice people. It's really great to see and I'm glad I'm part of this industry that seems so welcoming.
