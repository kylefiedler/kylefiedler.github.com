    On sketching

    Sketching was something that I did everyday in high school. I used to go through those hardcover hard bound sketch books. In high school, I carried it around with me in every class. When ever I had a chance I'd pull it out and jet start drawing. I pasted magazine articles, photos that I took and scraps of other thoughts and doodles. Each book would become a hodge podge of drawings sketches and various cut outs.
