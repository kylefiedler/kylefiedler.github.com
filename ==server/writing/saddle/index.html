Getting back on the saddle

It's been over a year since my last post. Over that time I've gotten engaged and married, started a new job (it's not new anymore), moved twice, started a business with a friend and done a ton of design. That’s a lot. It explains to me why something had to go. Unfortunately, it was this site

This will be my third attempt at maintaining a blog and the second time that I have abandoned one. It's not that during that time I didn't want to write for this site it's just that I didn't make time for it.

Insane

The definition of insanity is trying the same thing over and over and thinging that you'll get different results. Well that is pre icky what I am trying to do. For the third time I am restarting a blog expecting to actually stay with it this time. Crazy. Probably, but its something that I can just not let die. At least not yet.

This time like the last I am going to set out with some new goals and guidelines.

Schedule

Last time I tried to hold myself to a once a month post. This was perfect for me at the time. It held me to deadlines and made me write something that I hated to do and needed encouragement to do. I think tht it led to some posts that were rushed and subpar. Now I don't think that that is the case. I don't need that encouragement. I am not going to stick to any kind of schedule. I'll post when I have something to say.

Illustration

I want to add a little bit more of my personality into my posts and bassicaly just wanted an excuse to use my Wacom and draw.

Comments

For the most part, the comments on my blog were congratulatory, which are awesome, but I never had a real conversation in them. For now I'm not going to implement comments on my site. I am going to have a new email address set up so that perhaps if people want to comment they can have a more real conversation about the post. This could change.
